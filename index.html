Welcome to my new blog!

I am a computer tech who has owned and operated a repair shop since 2011.

I have learned a lot in that time and have decided it is time to start giving back to the online community in my own small way. I will be sharing my adventures in fixing computers by posting here on my GitHub page thanks to this excellent tutorial.

These posts are not meant to be tutorials or step-by-step guides. What they are is short articles about my process for working through a problem and arriving at a solution, including any background information that might be useful.

Since I have a lot of extra equipment to work with and multiple computers running different software I am able to experiment and troubleshoot, and hopefully this information will help others.
